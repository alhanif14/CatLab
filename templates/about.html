About This Project

Our project, CatLab, focuses on developing an innovative pet feeder that combines mobility with remote control capabilities and automatic feeding via a web interface. This advanced feeder mobility feature operates through an RC car, allowing users to navigate it effortlessly to their pets' location for feeding.


CatLab utilizes a programmed stepper motor to precisely control the amount of food dispensed, ensuring accurate portion sizes. Through a user-friendly web interface, pet owners can remotely adjust portions, and monitor the amount of food inside pet feeder.


This technology not only enhances convenience for pet owners but also promotes efficient pet care management, and ensuring pets receive accurate portion of food. CatLab aims to redefine pet feeding by integrating mobility, remote control, and precise portion management into a single innovative solution.
